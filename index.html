One of the biggest challenges when creating a game is ensuring that it is both fun and challenging. You need to find the right balance between the two so that players will keep coming back to play your game. Another challenge is creating the game mechanics themselves, such as character movement, collision detection, and scoring systems. In terms of programming, JavaScript can be a bit tricky to work with, especially when it comes to debugging. It's essential to make sure that your code is organized and easy to understand, as this will make it much easier to fix any bugs that crop up during development.

2) Shoot: Press the spacebar or left-click your mouse to fire lasers and take down the invading chickens.
3) Avoid enemy fire: The spaceship will fire back at you, so be sure to dodge their attacks and avoid taking damage.
